About Us

Bobby Mac

Lead Vocals, Guitar

Born in Dublin, Ireland in 1974.

This founder member has been instrumental in this bands success. His belting vocals have been filling stadiums around the world for 2 decades now and along with his mega guitar riffs, this artist is surely reaching iconic status!

Zz

Bass guitar

Born in Madrid, Spain in 1979.

Joined the lineup after the infamous bust-up with previous bassist Marko, Zz has added his own unique tempo to the bands sound and elevated them to true worldwide fame!

Joe M

Drums

Born in Dublin, Ireland in 1985

The baby of the band and his continuous thumping drumming will surely keep the band rocking for years to come!
